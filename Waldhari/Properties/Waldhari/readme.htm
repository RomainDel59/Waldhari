BUSINESS OPPORTUNITY OF A LIFETIME!

Want to build your criminal empire in Los Santos. Waldhari Corporation is relocating to Provence, France - undoubtedly the most beautiful country in the world. Time to grab their assets at rock-bottom prices. From legitimate businesses to more adventurous ventures, seize this opportunity to make your mark on the city.

Features
  • Cocaine Workshop
  • Methamphetamine Lab
  • Weed Farm
Required mods
  • ScriptHookVDotNet 3.6.0
  • LemonUI 2.1.2
  • iFruitAddon2 3.0.0
Installation Unzip in scripts folder.
Should be like this 'GTA exe folder'/scripts/Waldhari.dll.

Customization The mod offers a wide range of options to customize your gameplay experience. From adjusting production rates to tweaking pricing and supply settings, you have full control over the details.
Explore all the available options in the Wiki.
